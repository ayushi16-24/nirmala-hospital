With over 18 years of dedicated experience in women's healthcare, Dr. Nirmala Yaduvanshi has transformed the lives of thousands of patients through expert clinical care and advanced minimally invasive surgical techniques. Her practice is founded on compassion, precision, and a deep commitment to personalized patient care.
